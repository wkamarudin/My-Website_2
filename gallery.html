Success is Nothing Without Safety

Getting hurt or killed in order to achieve goal shall not be quoted at all.
Putting ourselves in risk without taking all the precautions is another form of stupidity. No exception.
Does not matter either the reward is in material or satisfaction form.
But it does not mean we should do things halfhearted. Understand every single detail, choose the best way, take all the necessary precaution.
Adhere and be strict with standard and procedure, maintain focus at the highest level, stand and execute the each step without compromise.
Keep improving and know exactly when we should stop and revisit.
Success is not a one way street. Protecting our safety and back to family in a complete piece is also a success that often rarely appreciated.
There is no point at all to put tears in our beloved family members.
Safety is not happened by accident.
It requires competency and commitment. When you gamble with safety, you bet your life.. and remember we only live once.
Be strong and be tough at work but at the same time respect the hazard and underlying threat and danger.
